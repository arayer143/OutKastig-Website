About Us

OutKast Industrial Group is a family owned and operated industrial decontamination company that specializes in above ground storage tanks (ASTs), pipeline systems, and process units as well as emergency response of chemical spills and fixed-facility system releases. With extensive proven experience, OutKast IG crews are able to perform on systems involving a wide range of hazardous chemicals.

    At OutKast Industrial Group, our mission is To offer turnkey solutions of decontamination processes for above ground storage tanks, pipeline systems, and process units. We are driven by a set of core values that define our work:

  • Quality
  • Integrity
  • Safety
  • innovation
